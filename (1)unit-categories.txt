Category 1 - Mechanical
The original work of creating Units. Founded technology to which was used everywhere.

Category 2 - Broken 
A sickening sight, broken counterparts of the Mechanical Category... they appear as if they had been
disoriented, torn and even mutated... one shuddered to imagine facing these as no external or internal
force could damage them. It was discovered that they die from their own decay, rotting away like a real
corpse... as one suggests it is wise to hold them off for as long as possible until they collapse from 
their own decay.

Category 3 - Bioengineered
A gruesome way to create "Lifeforms".. never to be a proper term for as they stand out at a sickening
appearance. Genetically engineered to be a superior version of the Mechanical Category but served as a
threat as it suddenly grew consciousness of its own.. as if  the [Plague] had discovered...

Category 4 - Mutated
The lost lifeform from the [Plague] and the intense dying radiation as the war breaks out. Many lives
were lost, many became anew... and those "anew" became more distorted, disfigured but far stronger...

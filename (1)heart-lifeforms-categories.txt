Category 1 - Active
A state of the heart that shows a bit of life but does not beat, RARELY found in medium-sized
or minir blood pools.. but never EVER touch these as to what they may offer inside of them.

Category 2 - Inactive
Where the heart has no activity and been in rest for centuries since then, maybe even presumed
dead. Untouchable by any means and is sometimes found in minor blood pools from what it made.

Category 3 - Dead
When the heart has long passed, surviving for thousands of years now decayed but still staying
at its shape. commonly BUT SOMETIMES rarely found in major or larger levels of blood pools.
